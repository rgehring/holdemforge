Hold Em Forge LLC

Hold Em Forge LLC has shut down

What was it?

Hold Em Forge was a poker analytics studio capable of calculating near-exact mathematical solutions for Bayesian, Epsilon-Safe adaptive and Nash strategies in certain poker scenarios.

Why shut down?

Changing enforcement policies for government regulation resulted in the closing of many poker sites serving US customers right around launch time for Hold Em Forge. While not affected directly, the industry tumult led to the decision to shutter the business.
